# JP Sada

JP Sada is the **Infrastructure & Data Engineering Lead** at the MCP Research Team, responsible for managing production systems, MLOps, and research into data leakage. His role emphasizes end-to-end ownership of infrastructure, ensuring the integrity and scalability of data pipelines critical to the team’s research workflows. Key areas of focus include [[concepts/mlops]], [[concepts/data-leakage]], and the integration of alternative data sources, with specific expertise in tools like AWS/S3, Airflow, Snowflake, and ETL processes [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]].

Sada’s responsibilities extend to overseeing research that addresses challenges in [[concepts/llm-temporal-leakage]], [[concepts/lookahead-bias]], and [[concepts/point-in-time-data]], ensuring robustness in backtesting and data ingestion. He serves as the primary routing target for infra-adjacent research topics, such as pipeline orchestration and alt-data plumbing, aligning with the team’s need for reliable and scalable systems [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]].

## Additional Research Interests

Recent discussions in the #ai Slack channel highlight Sada’s engagement with AI agent applications in financial markets. He has shared links to multi-agent research systems, such as Anthropic’s [[https://www.anthropic.com/engineering/multi-agent-research-system]] and Hugging Face’s ML Intern agent [[https://huggingface.co/spaces/smolagents/ml-intern]], suggesting exploration of agent-based approaches for research workflows. Additionally, he has shown interest in autonomous trading agents, as evidenced by his reference to Fere AI’s self-improving trading agent platform [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].
