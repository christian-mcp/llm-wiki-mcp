# LLM Wiki

The LLM Wiki is a collaborative research knowledge base designed to track and organize information related to large language models (LLMs) and their applications. It serves as a centralized repository for findings, concepts, and discussions within the AI research community, with integration into Slack channels for real-time updates and tracking of developments [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]]. This system enables users to document hypotheses, synthesize research, and link to external sources such as academic papers, industry reports, and open-source projects, facilitating cross-referencing and evidence-based analysis.

The LLM Wiki is particularly notable for its role in aggregating insights from Slack discussions, such as updates on AI-driven weather forecasting by NOAA, advancements in self-improving trading agents, and open-source tools like Hugging Face’s ML Intern. It connects to entities like [[entities/les-finemore]], [[entities/jp-sada]], and [[entities/noaa]], as well as concepts such as [[concepts/agentic-systems]] and [[concepts/ai-in-finance]], reflecting its interdisciplinary scope. By linking to specific facts (e.g., [[facts/noaa-aigfs-efficiency]]), the LLM Wiki ensures traceability of claims and supports evidence-based research [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].
