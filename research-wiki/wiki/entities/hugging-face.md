# Hugging Face

Hugging Face is a company known for its contributions to open-source AI and machine learning tools. In April 2026, the company launched the **ML Intern** program, an initiative aimed at advancing open-source AI education. This program includes the release of an open-source AI agent called ML Intern, which automates the post-training workflow for large language models (LLMs). The tool is designed to streamline tasks such as model evaluation, hyperparameter tuning, and deployment, making it easier for researchers and developers to iterate on LLMs. This aligns with Hugging Face's broader mission to democratize access to AI technologies through open-source collaboration [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].

The ML Intern agent is hosted on Hugging Face Spaces, a platform for sharing and deploying machine learning models and applications. By providing this tool as an open-source project, Hugging Face enables the AI community to contribute to and benefit from advancements in LLM training and optimization. The initiative reflects a growing trend in the AI field to develop infrastructure that supports both academic research and industry applications, particularly in areas like automated machine learning (AutoML) and reproducible AI workflows [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].
