# NOAA

NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) is a U.S. government agency focused on environmental monitoring and weather forecasting. In the context of AI research, NOAA has deployed the **AIGFS** (Artificial Intelligence Global Forecast System), an AI-driven replacement for the traditional Global Forecast System (GFS). This model achieves **99.7% less compute efficiency** compared to legacy systems while extending forecast skill by **18–24 hours** [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].

The AIGFS represents a significant advancement in weather modeling, with early verification data showing improved performance over the legacy GEFS ensemble. Its operational deployment is directly relevant to applications like weather-crop signal pipelines, particularly for extended-range growing-season forecasts [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]]. The model's output is now publicly accessible, enabling integration with other systems such as ECMWF ENS for enhanced forecasting capabilities.
