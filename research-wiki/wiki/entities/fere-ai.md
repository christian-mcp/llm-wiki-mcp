# Fere AI

Fere AI is a startup focused on developing autonomous AI agents for financial portfolio management. According to a report shared in the #ai Slack channel, the company raised $1.3M in a seed round led by [[entities/ethereal-ventures]], a venture capital firm focused on DeFi (decentralized finance) projects. The platform aims to deploy self-improving trading agents that manage financial portfolios through a continuous learning loop, positioning it as a crypto-adjacent project. This aligns with broader trends in [[concepts/self-improving-ai-agents]] and [[concepts/ai-in-finance]], though the direct relevance to traditional financial markets is described as low [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].

The funding announcement was highlighted in the Slack digest as an example of capital flowing into autonomous agent-based trading infrastructure. While the project’s immediate impact on financial markets may be limited, it reflects growing interest in [[concepts/agentic-systems]] for algorithmic trading. The source notes that the funding details were reported by the Manila Times / GlobeNewswire, though the Slack message itself serves as the primary reference for this information [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].
