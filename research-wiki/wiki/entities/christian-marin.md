# Christian Marin

Christian Marin is an expert in **agentic AI** and **local LLM deployment**, specializing in multi-agent systems, LLM pipeline orchestration, and on-prem inference. His work spans the development of agent frameworks, reinforcement learning applications, and tools for deploying large language models in non-cloud environments. Recent research assignments include projects like *Beyond Prompting*, *DatedGPT*, *DeltaHedge*, *OPHR*, and *MILLION*, which align with his focus on advancing agentic AI systems and improving LLM deployment infrastructure [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]].

Marin collaborates closely with Carlos on agentic AI topics, with Marin focusing on infrastructure and deployment while Carlos leans into finance-related applications. He is associated with tools such as [[entities/clawdbot]] and models like [[entities/qwen3]], reflecting his involvement in both practical deployment and cutting-edge research. His role notes emphasize a strong fit for research on LLM deployment and multi-agent reinforcement learning, positioning him as a key contributor to the team’s work on [[concepts/llm-pipeline-orchestration]] and [[concepts/agentic-ai]] [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]].
