## Summary
A routing guide for assigning research papers to team members based on expertise, workload, and focus areas. Profiles detail five researchers with specialized domains including derivatives, infrastructure, agentic AI, LLM deployment, and weather-commodities analysis.

## Key Takeaways
- Héctor Gómez curates derivatives pricing, risk modeling, and macro-FX research with a focus on volatility surfaces and commodities. [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]
- JP Sada oversees infrastructure, MLOps, and data pipeline integrity, prioritizing research on data leakage and alternative data systems. [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]
- Christian Marin specializes in local LLM deployment, multi-agent systems, and reinforcement learning, with recent work on DeltaHedge and OPHR. [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]
- Carlos focuses on LLMs for investing, anomalies, and factor research, collaborating with Christian on agentic AI but prioritizing finance applications. [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]
- Rafael Gallegos leads weather-commodities research using spatial-temporal ML, satellite data, and crop yield forecasting with a PhD-level expertise. [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]
- A cheat-sheet routes papers to specific team members based on keywords like 'derivatives', 'MLOps', or 'spatial-temporal ML'. [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]
