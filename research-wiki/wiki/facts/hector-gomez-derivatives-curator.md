# Héctor Gómez as Derivatives Curator

## Claim
Héctor Gómez serves as the primary curator for research related to **derivatives pricing**, **volatility surfaces**, and **risk modeling** within the MCP Research Team. This role includes acting as the first filter for incoming papers in these domains and overseeing research that intersects with commodities, FX hedging, and macro/FX crossovers into commodities [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]].

## Evidence
The source explicitly states that Héctor Gómez is the "natural 'curator' in the pipeline — first filter for new reads" and identifies him as the "default owner" for research involving derivatives pricing, volatility surfaces, risk models, backtesting, commodities, and FX hedging [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]. A quick routing cheat-sheet in the document further associates him with keywords such as "derivatives, vol, term structure, macro/FX, risk" [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]].

## Why It Matters
Héctor Gómez’s role as a curator ensures that research in critical areas like derivatives pricing and risk modeling is prioritized and triaged effectively. This structure helps the team allocate resources to high-impact work while maintaining expertise in complex domains such as volatility surfaces and factor investing [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]]. His oversight also extends to cross-cutting topics like commodities and FX, which are central to both risk management and trading strategies [[sources/mcp-research-team-paper-assignment-profiles]].
