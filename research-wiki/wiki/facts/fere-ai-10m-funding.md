# Fere AI's $1.3M Funding

## Claim
Fere AI raised $1.3M in a seed round for the development of self-improving trading agents, with the investment led by Ethereal Ventures [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].

## Evidence
The funding announcement was shared in the #ai Slack channel on April 23, 2026, by Les Finemore, citing a Manila Times / GlobeNewswire article. The source describes the round as being "led by Ethereal Ventures" for a platform where autonomous AI agents manage financial portfolios, with a self-improvement loop [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]]. The post also notes that Ethereal Ventures is DeFi-focused, and the project is described as "crypto-adjacent" [[sources/slack-digest-ai-channel-updates-april-2026]].

## Why It Matters
This funding highlights growing interest in autonomous agent-based trading infrastructure, particularly in the context of self-improving AI systems [[concepts/self-improving-ai-agents]]. The mention of Ethereal Ventures ties the development to DeFi ecosystems, which may influence the broader adoption of AI in finance [[concepts/ai-in-finance]]. While the direct relevance to MCP (a financial analytics entity) is noted as limited, the funding underscores trends in AI-driven trading platforms [[entities/mcp]].
